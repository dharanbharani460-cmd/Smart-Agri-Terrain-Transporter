The Smart Agri-Terrain Transporter is a low-cost agricultural cart designed to help farmers transport crops on rough and hilly terrain. 
The system uses a DC motor, gear reduction mechanism, and traction wheels to move efficiently on uneven surfaces.

The prototype was tested for load carrying, hill climbing, and rough terrain movement. The results showed that the cart successfully transported crops while reducing manual effort for farmers.

This project provides a practical and affordable solution for agricultural transportation in rural and hilly regions.
