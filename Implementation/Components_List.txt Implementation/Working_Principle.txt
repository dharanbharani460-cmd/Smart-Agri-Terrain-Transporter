The Smart Agri-Terrain Transporter is designed as a compact agricultural cart for transporting crops on rough terrain. The system consists of a strong steel chassis frame that supports all components. 
A DC motor provides the driving force for the vehicle. The motor is connected to a gear reduction system which increases torque to allow the cart to climb slopes.

The power from the gear system is transmitted to the wheels through a chain and sprocket mechanism. The wheels are designed with deep traction patterns so the vehicle can move on muddy and uneven farm paths. 
A cargo tray is mounted on the chassis to carry harvested crops safely during transportation.

The design focuses on simplicity, durability, and low cost so that farmers can easily use and maintain the system.
